We aim to make Raghu Institute of Technology a convenient place for quality education and I am confident that we are on the right track of the ideology. RIT facilitates an atmosphere for the students in which they enjoy the process of learning and training. The amicable ambiance in RIT makes it easier for the student to have a regular rapport with the faculty members which, in turn, enhances the quality of education.

Sports

RIT campus having a national level cricket stadium has rich games and sports culture. The management of the college encourages the students who are selected to the Inter-university, All India Inter-University and National level games and sports by providing financial support. The fruits of the encouragement shows the in the selections and participation our students in national level events such as Swimming, Basketball, Football, Table Tennis, Volleyball, Cricket, Kickboxing, Rollersports And Lawn Tennis. RIT conducts Principal Trophy in the odd semester and Chairman Trophy in the even semester for all sports and games. Having spacious play grounds, RIT encourages sports and games such as cricket, volleyball, basket-ball, Kho-Kho, Lawn Tennis, Table Tennis, Badminton, Kabaddi, Handball, Football, Throw-Ball and Athletics etc.

NCC

The NCC army (2 Andhra girls battalion) has been instituted for girls in order to instill discipline and patriotism among the students. The NCC Army wing (boys) has been sanctioned form the academic year 2016-17. For boys NCC Air Force wing is available in open category.

Transportations

Raghu Institution of Technology provides and ensures a safe, comfortable, and time-bound bus-transportation facility that is well connected to every corner of Visakhapatnam and Vizianagaram districts as well as to Srikakulam town. Separate buses and timings for the first-year students as a part of safety and security measurements.
